# FitRPG - Gamified Fitness & Nutrition Mobile App

A comprehensive mobile application that transforms health and fitness tracking into an engaging RPG-style gaming experience.

### **Dashboard/Home Page**
- **Layout**: Scrollable overview with widget-style sections
- **User Avatar**: Current level display with XP progress bar
- **Achievement Badges**: Horizontal carousel showing recent unlocks
- **Quick Stats**: Daily calories, workouts completed, streak counter
- **Today's Workout Preview**: One-tap access to current workout plan
- **Meal Plan Summary**: Overview of daily nutrition goals
- **Progress Rings**: Visual representation of daily goal completion
- **Motivational Messages**: Dynamic content based on user activity

### **Calculators Page**
- **Tabbed Interface**: 9 essential fitness calculators
  - Calories Calculator with daily intake tracking
  - Hydration Calculator with personalized water goals
  - Sleep Needs Calculator based on activity and health goals
  - Macronutrients Calculator with customizable ratios
  - One Rep Max Calculator for strength progression
  - Basal Metabolism Calculator with activity multipliers
- **Real-time Calculations**: Auto-update as users input data
- **Results History**: Save and favorite previous calculations
- **Explanatory Output**: Clear numerical results with recommended actions

### **Daily Workout Page**
- **Structured Display**: Exercise cards with progression tracking
- **Exercise Details**: Name, target muscles, sets/reps/weight, difficulty
- **Interactive Features**: 
  - Animated exercise demonstrations
  - Progress tracking checkboxes
  - Integrated rest timer with play/pause/reset
- **Navigation**: Previous/next exercise buttons
- **Completion Celebration**: XP rewards and achievement unlocks

### **Daily Diet Page**
- **Meal-based Sections**: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks
- **Expandable Cards**: Detailed ingredient lists and preparation instructions
- **Macro Breakdown**: Visual protein, carbs, fats distribution
- **Progress Tracking**: Daily macro and calorie goal progress bars
- **Interactive Features**:
  - Meal substitution options
  - Shopping list generation
  - Cooking instructions with step-by-step guidance

### **Food Scanner Page**
- **Camera Interface**: Real-time food recognition with scanning overlay
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Photo capture with nutritional breakdown
- **Results Display**: 
  - Food identification confidence score
  - Detailed macro and calorie information
  - Adjustable portion sizes
- **Fallback Options**: Manual food search and barcode scanning
- **Integration**: Save directly to meal log

### **Progress Page**
- **Character Progression**: Level, XP, class, and rank display
- **Achievement Gallery**: Unlocked badges with completion dates
- **Statistical Charts**: Weight, body fat, strength gains visualization
- **Goal Tracking**: Visual progress toward fitness milestones
- **Streak Counters**: Daily, weekly, and monthly consistency tracking

### **Settings Page**
- **Categorized Organization**: 
  - Account Management (profile editing, password change)
  - App Preferences (units, language, theme)
  - Notifications (workout reminders, meal alerts)
  - Data & Privacy (analytics, sync settings)
- **Accessibility Features**: Proper contrast, text sizing, screen reader support
- **Customization Options**: Toggle switches, dropdowns, sliders
